The following are tips to start a blog

Do you ever feel excited to start a blog or share something on social media, yet find yourself unsure about which types of content creation best fit your vision?
With new formats emerging almost daily, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed by the sheer variety of options—from simple text posts to highly engaging social media content.
But here’s the good news: you don’t need to guess your way through.
In this guide, you’ll find over 100 types of content you can explore and refine to match your unique style or brand.
Whether you aim to produce high-quality written pieces, captivating videos, or interactive posts, there’s a format here for you.
By the end, you’ll be equipped with fresh ideas and a clearer sense of direction—so you can confidently share content that resonates with your audience and brings your creative vision to life.

Tips for Blog

Determine whether they’re actually a direct competitor.
A blog’s audience, niche, and specific slant determine whether they're your competitor.
The most important of these is their audience. If they serve a different public than you, then they’re likely not a competitor.
Look at the blog’s branding, color palette, and theme.
Colors and themes play a huge role in fitting into your niche.
For example, a blog about eco-friendly products should likely use earthy tones instead of loud, unnatural colors.
